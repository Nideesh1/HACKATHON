INSURANCE POLICY GUIDELINES - EFFECTIVE 2024

SECTION 1: PRIOR AUTHORIZATION REQUIREMENTS

The following procedures require prior authorization before service:
- All inpatient hospital admissions (except emergency)
- MRI and CT scans (non-emergency)
- Cardiac stress tests and echocardiograms
- All surgical procedures over $5,000
- Specialty medications and biologics
- Durable medical equipment over $500
- Physical therapy beyond 12 sessions
- Mental health inpatient treatment

Failure to obtain prior authorization may result in claim denial or reduced reimbursement.



SECTION 2: COVERAGE EXCLUSIONS

The following services are NOT covered under standard plans:
- Cosmetic surgery and procedures
- Infertility treatments (IVF, IUI) unless rider purchased
- Experimental or investigational treatments
- Weight loss surgery (unless BMI > 40 or BMI > 35 with comorbidities)
- Dental services (separate dental plan required)
- Vision correction surgery (LASIK, PRK)
- Services rendered outside network without referral



SECTION 3: ANNUAL LIMITS

Certain services have annual maximums:
- Spinal injections: Maximum 3 per calendar year
- Physical therapy: 30 sessions per year
- Chiropractic care: 20 visits per year
- Mental health outpatient: 52 sessions per year
- Hearing aids: One pair every 3 years



SECTION 4: EMERGENCY SERVICES

Emergency room visits are covered at 80% after deductible when:
- Patient presents with symptoms that a prudent layperson would consider emergency
- Services are rendered at nearest available facility
- Patient is admitted or observation status within 24 hours

Non-emergency use of ER will be subject to additional $250 copay.



SECTION 5: APPEALS PROCESS

Members may appeal denied claims within 180 days of denial notice.
Required documentation for appeals:
- Letter of medical necessity from treating physician
- Relevant medical records and test results
- Peer-reviewed literature supporting treatment (if applicable)

First-level appeals are reviewed within 30 days.
Second-level appeals require external review and take up to 45 days.



SECTION 6: PROVIDER NETWORK

In-Network Benefits:
- Preventive care: 100% covered
- Primary care visits: $25 copay
- Specialist visits: $50 copay
- Hospital admission: 80% after deductible

Out-of-Network Benefits:
- Subject to higher deductible
- Reimbursed at 60% of allowed amount
- Balance billing may apply
